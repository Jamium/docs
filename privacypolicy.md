# Privacy Promise

## Let's start with the basics
Your data is yours. Any information collected about you, for example, but not limited to: Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, your device, or real world approximate locations, are private.

### Your data will never be sold, shared, given to, or handled by any other entity in any way, shape, or form.
We do not approve of targeted ads, or anything related, as we believe it is our job to protect your data, rather than using it against you in hopes of getting you to spend money.

### Passwords aren't great
But they're important. Until every device has easy-to-use biometric sensors, and every site supports them, passwords are necessary. Remember to use different passwords for every site - including our sites, and use a good, trustworthy password manager to keep them all safe.
